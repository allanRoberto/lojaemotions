== Description ==


<h2>Social Testimonials and Reviews Plugin</h2>
<p>Kudobuzz Testimonial Widget plugin allows you to collect and display <b>social testimonials</b>  or selected reviews on your website.</p>
<p>Kudobuzz grabs all the positive and great things people are saying about you on social media(Facebook,Twitter,Instagram,Yelp,G+,Emails...) and allows you to display it on your website with an easy to customize clean widget. You can also collect business and product reviews.</p>
<p>With Kudobuzz you always get up-to-date <b>testimonials</b> on your website, this will wow your web visitors and improve your SEO drastically.</p>

1. Collecting testimonials from

	- Emails

2. Add personal testimonials

3. Set up an SEO Page

== Installation ==

1. Upload `Kudobux-testimonial-widget` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Visit the Kudobuzz Menu and start to customize your Kudos
